A textile manufacturer built for hospitals, not a uniform shop built for margins.

Carekraft was built on a simple observation: most hospital apparel is manufactured for cost, not for the people who wear it through a twelve-hour shift. We manufacture nursing uniforms, OT scrubs, doctor aprons and hospital linens with medical-grade fabric and reinforced construction, at the volumes institutions actually need.

From a single ward rollout to a multi-city hospital chain, our production, branding and logistics run under one roof — so what a hospital specifies is exactly what arrives.
